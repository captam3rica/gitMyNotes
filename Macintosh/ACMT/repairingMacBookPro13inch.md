## Ports

### MBP 13-inch, Mid 2012

- MagSafe
- Gigabit Ethernet
- FireWire 800
- Thunderbolt
- USB 3.0
- SDXC Card Slot
- Headphone Out/Optical Digital Audio Out
- Battery Indicator Light (BIL) Button

### MBP 13-inch Late 2013 - Early 2015

- MagSafe 2
- Thunderbolt (Late 2012, Early 2013); Thunderbolt 2 (Late 2013-Early 2015)
- USB 3.0
- Headphone (Audio Out)
- Microphone
- SDXC Card Slot
- HDMI
- USB 3.0
